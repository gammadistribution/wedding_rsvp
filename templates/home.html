Our Love Story

Adam and Jacqueline met back in October 2010 at a local Irish pub around the corner from Jacqueline’s college in Baltimore, Maryland. Adam made Jacqueline laugh, and Jacqueline made Adam happy.

During that same year, Jacqueline fell for Adam during the six-hour car ride up to Ohio for Thanksgiving. A few months later, Adam and Jacqueline spent a week together in Italy while Jacqueline studied in Rome. While seeing the sights and trying almost every flavor of gelato known to man, Adam and Jacqueline realized that they had found the person that they had waited for so patiently.

Adam started his Masters at UMBC, Jacqueline graduated from Loyola ready to start law school, and they moved to Elkridge, Maryland. Both Adam and Jacqueline began to build their house into a home and experienced a few bumps along the way. They will never forget the great flood of 2013 and the frogs that followed.

Adam and Jacqueline worked hard and played hard, too. Both loved trying new restaurants, taking a few memorable vacations, and spending time with family and friends. On the weekends, Adam and Jacqueline looked forward to TiVo time on the couch, Saturday morning gym dates, and the Ravens. Baltimore will always be their favorite city and a place they call home.

In October 2013, Adam took Jacqueline to dinner at the same Irish pub where they had first met. Adam drove Jacqueline to the place on Loyola’s campus where he first kissed her and asked Jacqueline to marry him. She said yes, and this year, Adam and Jacqueline will get married in the city where it all started.
